# WeChat Official Account Copy Rewrite / wechat-rewrite

---

## Introduction

Turn any draft into high-readership WeChat official-account copy—hook-driven titles, bold voice, and automatic integration of platform-specific keywords, memorable lines, and engagement prompts.

**Core Value**

- **One-step style conversion**: Output as the author, shifting entirely to a conversational, sharp WeChat tone
- **Viral structure**: Titles capped at 20 characters; body follows proven hook openers, bullet structures, and closing engagement prompts
- **Platform expression**: 2–3 keywords intelligently selected from 10 high-frequency WeChat terms, woven naturally into the copy for stronger platform fit
- **Ready to publish**: Title + body + 5–10 hashtag tags, delivered as finished copy

**Who It's For**

- ✍️ **WeChat account owners** — Quickly turn source material into publish-ready viral posts
- 📱 **Content creators** — Adapt notes, speeches, and more into copy optimized for WeChat reading
- 🏢 **Brand / content operations** — Align outbound messaging with WeChat style to boost opens and engagement

## Usage Guide

Simply describe your needs in natural language—no commands to memorize.

### Quick Reference

| Intent              | Example phrase                                                | Result                                       |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Rewrite a full post | "Rewrite this in WeChat viral style: [your draft]"            | Title + body with engagement prompt and tags |
| Title only          | "Give me a WeChat viral title under 20 characters for this"   | Hook-driven title                            |

- A closing engagement prompt
- 5–10 hashtag tags
