# WeChat Viral Copy Rewriter / wechat-article-style

---

## Overview

Turn any draft into WeChat official-account copy built for reads—hook-driven titles, bold voice, and natural platform-style wording.

**Core Value**

- **One-step style conversion**: Output as the author; keep the original paragraph structure while shifting to conversational, direct WeChat tone
- **Viral structure**: Titles within 20 characters; body follows proven openers, bullet structure, and closing engagement prompts
- **Platform keywords**: Weave in 2–3 terms such as guide, tutorial, or playbook naturally for stronger platform fit
- **Readable and shareable**: Memorable lines, light emoji, and closing tags for easier reading and sharing

**Intended Users**

- ✍️ WeChat operators — Turn source material into publish-ready viral-style posts quickly
- 📱 Creators — Adapt notes or scripts into copy that reads well on WeChat
- 🏢 Brand / marketing teams — Align outbound messaging with WeChat style to improve opens and engagement

---

## Features

### Core Capabilities

- **Style rewrite**: Provide your source text; get a WeChat-style title and body delivered as finished copy (no back-and-forth confirmation)
- **Viral titles**: Number-led, question, or exclamation hooks; title length capped at 20 characters
- **Body structure**: Pain-point openers, bullet sections, contrast; closing comment prompts plus 5–10 relevant tags
- **Built-in principles**: Emphasize relevance and practical value, clear stance, emotional resonance—avoid dry lecturing

## Usage Guide

Paste or describe the copy you want rewritten—no commands to memorize.

### Quick Reference

| Intent | Example phrase | Result |
| ------ | -------------- | ------ |
| Rewrite a full piece | "Rewrite this in WeChat viral style: [your draft]" | Title + body with engagement prompt and tags |
| Title only | "Give me a WeChat viral title under 20 characters for this" | Hook-driven title |
| Stronger tone | "Make it sharper with more memorable lines" | Bolder stance within viral structure |
| Conversational tone | "More casual, like chatting with readers" | Friendly, short-sentence WeChat voice |
