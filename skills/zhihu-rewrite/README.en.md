# Zhihu Copy Rewrite / zhihu-rewrite

---

## Introduction

Turn product intros, how-tos, or campaign notices into Zhihu-style copy—clear viewpoints, structured prose, and natural use of platform-flavored terms like guide, tutorial, or playbook.

**Core Value**

- **Credible, professional tone**: Unique angles and clear structure aligned with high-performing Zhihu content
- **Signature keywords**: Weave in 2–3 terms such as guide, tutorial, case study, or tips naturally in the body
- **Complete delivery**: One pass gives a hook title, conversational body, and closing `#` tags
- **Paragraph alignment**: Keeps the same paragraph hierarchy as your source for easy review

**Who It’s For**

- 💡 **Zhihu creators** — Turn source material into answer- or article-ready copy quickly
- 📱 **Content ops** — Adapt copy from other platforms to Zhihu’s depth-oriented readers
- 🏢 **Brand / marketing** — Professional outward messaging with consistent tags

---

## Features

### Core Capabilities

- **Zhihu-style rewrite**: Input source text; get a titled Zhihu-style piece
- **Conversational voice**: Friendly, fluent narration—avoid stiff formal prose
- **Platform keywords**: Pick 2–3 from guide, tutorial, playbook, experience share, case study, etc.
- **Topic tags**: Closing `#` tags tied to the content

---

## Usage Guide

Paste or describe the copy you want rewritten.

### Quick Reference

| Intent              | Example phrase                                                              | Result                                      |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Full rewrite        | “Rewrite this in Zhihu style: [your draft]”                                 | Title + body + tags                         |
| Product intro       | “This headset has ANC and 30-hour battery—make it Zhihu seeding copy”      | Professional yet conversational + keywords  |
| Campaign            | “Anniversary sale—50% off—rewrite for Zhihu”                                | Attention-grabbing title, approachable body |
| Stronger depth      | “Sharper viewpoint, clearer structure”                                      | More assertive, readable Zhihu-style piece  |

### Output Example

After rewriting, you’ll get content roughly like:

```text
# Title (hook-friendly for Zhihu feeds)

Body paragraph one: conversational, with guide/tutorial-style terms …
Body paragraph two: clear structure, explicit viewpoint …

#tech #headphones #review
```
