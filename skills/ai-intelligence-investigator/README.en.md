# A-Share Intelligence Investigator / ai-intelligence-investigator

---

## Overview

A deep A-share intelligence investigation and analysis tool that helps you quickly complete company research, competitive comparison, event tracking, background checks, and information verification, delivering structured investigation reports to support investment decisions, competitive analysis, sentiment tracking, and more.

**Core Value**

- **Comprehensive coverage**: A-share company fundamentals, industry chains, concept trends, capital flows, research reports, and sentiment dynamics—all in one place
- **Credibility annotations**: Investigation results come with credibility ratings distinguishing confirmed, unconfirmed, and denied information, so you can judge reliability at a glance
- **Structured delivery**: Outputs complete investigation reports with data, sources, and credibility annotations—ready for decision-making
- **Multi-scenario support**: A-share intelligence, competitive analysis, event tracking, background checks, and information verification—one tool for multiple investigation needs

**Intended Users**

- 📈 **Investors** — Quickly access comprehensive analysis of A-share company fundamentals, industry landscape, and capital flows
- 🏢 **Product managers / marketers** — Systematically understand competitor features, user feedback, and market performance
- 📰 **Content ops / journalists** — Track event timelines and grasp multi-perspective sentiment trends
- 🤝 **Business development / investors** — Pre-deal background checks and reputation assessment
- 🔍 **Everyone** — Verify online information and confirm key facts

---

## Features

### Core Capabilities

- **A-share intelligence investigation**: Gathers comprehensive information on listed companies including financial reports, announcements, research notes, industry policies, capital flows, and forum sentiment; outputs structured reports spanning company overview, financials, industry analysis, concept heat, capital dynamics, institutional views, and risk alerts
- **Competitive intelligence investigation**: Multi-dimensional analysis of competitors across product features, user feedback, and market performance on domestic and international platforms
- **Sentiment & event investigation**: Reconstructs event timelines, collects multi-party perspectives, and assesses credibility of key information
- **Background investigation**: Verifies basic information, validates professional background, and screens for controversies and reputation risks
- **Information cross-validation**: Compares multiple sources on the same claim, delivering confirmed / unconfirmed / denied conclusions with bias analysis
- **Credibility annotations**: Rates investigation findings with credibility levels (✅ confirmed / ⚠️ unconfirmed / ❌ denied / 🔍 single-source) so you can quickly judge information reliability

### Highlights

- **Comprehensive intelligence coverage**: Covers company fundamentals, industry chains, concept sectors, capital flows, research ratings, and other core dimensions
- **Transparent credibility**: Every key piece of information is annotated with its source and credibility level, so you know exactly what's reliable and what needs further verification
- **A-share deep focus**: Purpose-built for the A-share market, with deep coverage of specialized financial information sources
