# Xiaohongshu Copy Rewrite / xiaohongshu-rewrite

---

## Introduction

Turn product intros, experience notes, or how-to drafts into Xiaohongshu (RED)–style posts—catchy titles, rich emoji, and closing topic tags that read like a real creator sharing.

**Core Value**

- **Platform-native voice**: Conversational, rhythmic copy aligned with common seeding and sharing patterns on RED
- **Visual readability**: Emoji in titles and body for energy and scroll-stopping appeal (at least 5 unique emoji)
- **Ready to post**: One pass delivers title + body + `#` tags for note publishing
- **Structure preserved**: Keeps your original paragraph layout while switching style, so edits stay easy

**Who It’s For**

- 📝 **RED creators** — Polish rough drafts into publishable note copy quickly
- 🛍️ **Brand / e-commerce ops** — Align product selling points with RED tone and tags
- 📱 **Cross-platform creators** — Adapt WeChat, Zhihu, or other long-form text into short RED notes

---

## Features

### Core Capabilities

- **RED-style rewrite**: Input source text; get a titled Xiaohongshu-style piece
- **Hook titles**: Suspense and contrast patterns common in high-performing note titles
- **Emoji throughout**: At least 5 non-repeating emoji across title and body
- **Topic tags**: Closing `#` tags strongly tied to the content for discovery

---

## Usage Guide

Paste or describe the copy you want rewritten.

### Quick Reference

| Intent           | Example phrase                                                      | Result                                      |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Full note rewrite| “Rewrite this in Xiaohongshu style: [your draft]”                   | Title + body + closing tags                 |
| Product seeding  | “This serum focuses on repair—make it a RED seeding post”         | Conversational benefits + relevant tags     |
| How-to share     | “Weekend city-walk route—turn it into a RED guide note”             | Clear structure, rich emoji, note-friendly  |
| Stronger title   | “Make the title punchier; keep the RED voice in the body”           | Sharper hook, still platform-appropriate    |

### Output Example

After rewriting, you’ll get content roughly like:

```text
# Title (with emoji, attention-grabbing)

Body paragraph one ✨ …
Body paragraph two 💡 …
(at least 5 unique emoji in full text)

#skincare #musthave #seeding
```
