# WeChat Viral Article Hunter / wechat-10w-hot

---

## Introduction

Pinpoint viral articles with **100k+** reads. This tool breaks down what makes WeChat Official Account hits work across the web—not only a daily **100k+** leaderboard, but data-backed insight into what drives share-worthy content.

**Feature Overview**

**Capture viral articles in one place**: Solves slow, scattered viral-article search. Collects the **TOP 50** **100k+** articles across industries daily so you can see where top traffic is going.

**Lock in your niche**: Deep industry segmentation with search and subscription for **100k+** content in specific fields—stay on top of competitors and keep topics on track.

**Decode viral patterns**: Automatically breaks down hooks, narrative structure, posting timing, and more—turning intuition into a repeatable creation model.

**Immersive reading & export**: Near-native WeChat-style page reading, with one-click **PDF** export for offline team reviews and deep dives.

**Core Value**

- **What you see is the list**: Rankings by category and time window in chat, plus a shareable visual page.
- **Grounded in data**: Updated daily; if fewer than 10 items exist, the list shows what’s actually available—no padding.
- **Patterns you can reuse**: Viral-pattern analysis based on real entries, plus niche subscriptions when the product supports them.

**Who It’s For**

- 📝 **WeChat editors / operators** — Track trends, titles, and account mix; find **100k+** supply fast.
- 📊 **Content leads** — Shareable, printable ranking pages for team reviews.
- 📈 **Growth / business teams** — Monitor competitors and niche **100k+** viral content.

## How to Use

Describe what you need in plain language—no fixed commands to memorize.

### Quick Phrase Guide

| Intent | Example prompt | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Today’s viral hits | “What are today’s 100k+ viral articles?” | Latest available overall ranking |
| Niche track | “Tech 100k+ hits from the past week” | Category + time-window ranking |
| More items | “Show all—give me 50 entries” | Longer list in the same window |
| Shareable page | “Generate a ranking page I can export as PDF” | Visual page after the in-chat list |
| Subscribe | “Subscribe me to daily finance viral picks” | Subscription flow after analysis (if enabled) |

### Sample Output

After a query, you first see the **full ranking** (notes, overview, and details), then a **viral-pattern summary**, and a **visual ranking page** with **PDF** export for sharing and offline reading.
